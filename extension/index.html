Video access renewal plans

Use the appropriate subscription plan to extend your video access. Please ensure that your access has expired or is about to expire so you avoid subscribing for an unnecessary service, if you are unsure about this feel free to contact customer service.

Depending on the specific course you will need to select a type of course.

In general courses are split in two categories: Essential(which includes Office professional, Basic computers, and English) and Growth (such as Software development, IT, Architecture etc.)

You can manage active subscriptions in the Subscriptions tab
